# Study Preferences

Project: AI
Updated: 2026-06-13

## Explanation Preferences

- Preferred language: Chinese, with English technical terms when useful.
- Preferred style: concrete, practical, ROI-oriented, not abstract or generic.
- Required concept template: what it is, core theory, how to use it, how it helps life/decisions/learning/work, how it improves productivity/thinking, ROI-max action.
- Difficulty: deep enough to eat through the concept, but explained at the user's current level.

## Motivation Patterns

- Strong driver: daily return to Codex.
- Strong driver: visible output and weekly scoring.
- Avoid: long passive reading without active recall or artifact.
- Avoid: heavy Notion maintenance that does not improve learning.
