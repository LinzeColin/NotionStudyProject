# Top 1% Strategy

## Definition

Top 1% in an arXiv archive does not mean reading every paper. It means building enough structure to judge what matters, what is noise, what is frontier, and what can transfer into real decisions or systems.

## Core Abilities

| Ability | Definition |
|---|---|
| Archive map | Know the field boundaries, subproblems, methods, and neighboring archives |
| Paper triage | Quickly distinguish landmark, incremental, flawed, and irrelevant papers |
| Concept depth | Explain core concepts in plain language and technical language |
| Method literacy | Understand the standard models, assumptions, metrics, and failure modes |
| Frontier radar | Track what is changing and why it matters |
| ROI transfer | Convert research into strategy, tools, workflows, experiments, or investment judgment |

## Anti-Patterns

- Reading papers sequentially without a concept map.
- Collecting PDFs without extracting reusable models.
- Treating every new paper as equally important.
- Confusing mathematical difficulty with decision value.
- Starting tools before understanding the archive's core questions.
