## Goal

Build CEO/CFO-level commercial, legal, financial, trading, and opportunity-screening judgment.

This project consolidates:

| Area | Role |
|---|---|
| Business strategy / analysis / acumen | Decide what is worth building, buying, selling, or avoiding. |
| Startup CEO legal basics | Understand legal risk categories enough to ask better questions and avoid obvious mistakes. |
| Investment / finance / trading / stocks / futures | Build market judgment, evidence discipline, risk boundaries, and execution awareness. |
| Web-wide arbitrage opportunities | Build an opportunity-sourcing and validation framework, not random opportunity chasing. |
| Local strategy simulator | Connect finance ideas to testable simulation and evidence. |

## Important Boundary

This is education and decision-framework building, not legal, financial, or trading advice. Any real trading or legal action requires jurisdiction-specific professional review and explicit execution controls.

## Success Criteria

- Can frame business opportunities using market, customer, unit economics, competition, moat, and execution risk.
- Can identify legal risk categories: company obligations, contracts, IP, privacy, employment, consumer/fair trading, director duties.
- Can reason about trading/arbitrage opportunities with evidence, costs, liquidity, risk, and execution limits.
- Can produce a CEO/CFO decision memo and a risk-controlled opportunity scorecard.
